name: openoffice-impress-templates layout: post title: OpenOffice Impress Templates time: 2008-11-27 00:32:00 +02:00 Turns out there are not many templates that come with OpenOffice.org Impress, which is used for creating presentations. Check out presentation helper, which has really good free presentation designs.

To go along with that, OpenOffice.org Wiki page has the instructions for enabling them on Impress.

Click on File->Templates->Organize and then add them using Command->Import Template.
